Buyer questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need Notion?

No. The package also includes downloadable files. Notion is only required if you want to use the live duplicate-link template.

How do I duplicate the template?

Open the live Notion page and click `Duplicate page`, then save it into your own workspace.

Can I edit it?

Yes. Duplicate it into your own workspace first, then edit your copy however you want.

Is this legal/financial/job advice?

No. This does not provide job, interview, salary, or hiring guarantees.

What if I only want the downloadable files?

That is fine. You can ignore the live template and use the downloadable files only.
